⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. All your data will be removed and cannot be recovered.

📖 Overview

You have the right to delete your Physio Therapist account at any time. Once your account is deleted, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

📝 How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account using either of the following methods:

Method 1: From the App

  1. Open the Physio Therapist app on your device.
  2. Navigate to Settings from the bottom navigation or side menu.
  3. Tap on "Account" or "Profile Settings".
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  5. Confirm your identity by entering your password.
  6. Tap "Confirm Delete" to permanently delete your account.

Method 2: Via Email

Send an email to mahendrakandu@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

📊 Data That Gets Deleted vs. Retained

Upon account deletion, the following data will be handled as described:

Permanently Deleted Retained (Legal/Business)
Profile Information
Health & Treatment Data
In-App Messages
Exercise & Progress Records
Appointment History
Location Preferences
Payment Transactions*
Legal Compliance Data*

* Payment transaction records and legal compliance data may be retained for up to 5 years as required by applicable financial and tax regulations.

⏱️ Deletion Timeline

Immediately: Your account is deactivated and you are logged out.

Within 24 hours: Your profile becomes invisible to other users.

Within 7 days: Active data begins purging from primary systems.

Within 30 days: All personal data is permanently removed from backups.

🔄 Before You Delete

Please consider the following before proceeding:

  • All your treatment history, exercise records, and progress data will be permanently lost.
  • Any active appointments will be automatically cancelled.
  • You will not be able to recover your account or data after deletion.
  • If you have any pending payments or refunds, please resolve them before deleting your account.
  • You can create a new account with the same email address after deletion, but previous data will not be restored.
